## Predictive Labs (his company)

Predictive Labs builds AI systems for European public services and enterprises that are
"auditable by design and open where they can be." It operates across the UK, Nordics,
Benelux and Baltics. Core capabilities:

1. Document Intelligence — extraction and retrieval from regulatory filings, clinical
   protocols and legal documents with auditable citations.
2. Applied Forecasting — demand, revenue and operational projections combining internal
   data with open sources and satellite signals.
3. Geospatial & Mobility — origin–destination matrices and anomaly detection on open data.
4. Agentic Workflows — multi-step LLM agents operating within client security boundaries.

Focus sectors: defense & public security, health & life sciences, public management &
mobility, and financial services (its commercial root, ~20% of work).

Notable delivered projects:
- Denmark (Health): privacy-preserving pipeline for real-world cancer medicine validation,
  reducing follow-up cycles from nine months to three months.
- UK (Public Management): county-wide traffic analytics replacing commercial GPS licensing
  with gravity models and open data.
- Microsoft: enterprise RAG systems for energy, biotech and financial-services clients.
